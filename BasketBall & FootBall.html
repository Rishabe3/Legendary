Football



Association football, more commonly known as simply football or soccer, is a team sport played between two teams of 11 players who primarily use their feet to propel the ball around a rectangular field called a pitch. The objective of the game is to score more goals than the opposition by moving the ball beyond the goal line into the opposing side's rectangular framed goal. Traditionally, the game has been played over two 45 minute halves, for a total match time of 90 minutes. The football world cup(fifa world cup) is played internationaly. There is also a premier league, which is a contested between 20 clubs. With an estimated 250 million players active in over 200 countries, it is considered the world's most popular sport.
